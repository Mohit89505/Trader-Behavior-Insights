## Objective
Analyze the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment (Fear & Greed Index) and trader performance.

## Datasets
- Historical Trader Data (Hyperliquid)
- Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index

## Key Insights
- Extreme Greed phases show higher risk and volatility
- Fear phases show lower leverage and conservative trading
- Sentiment-aware strategies can improve risk management

## Tools
Python, Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib
